Beneath soft skies in pastel hue,
She writes her world in shades she knew.
A whisper light, a fearless spark,
A glowing soul within the dark.

From tangled hair and sleepy eyes,
To late-night thoughts and quiet cries,
She builds herself in silent grace,
A universe no one can replace.

Her mirror knows the highs and lows,
The dreams she hides, the strength she shows.
A swipe of gloss, a liner wing,
Armor for everything she’ll bring.

She walks through crowds, yet stands apart,
With fragile strength and lion’s heart.
A storm disguised in calmest face,
A wildfire wrapped in gentle grace.

Her playlist hums her secret tales,
Of broken dreams and love that pales.
Of healing slow, of learning how
To choose herself, here and now.

She loves in depth, she feels too much,
A simple glance, a healing touch.
She gives the world her golden light,
Even when she’s lost the fight.

Her laughter rings like windchimes free,
A melody of what she’ll be.
Each scar she holds, a story told,
Of battles faced and growing bold.

She paints her nails in shifting shades,
A thousand moods, a masquerade.
Soft pink days and midnight blues,
Each color holds a different muse.

Her texts are poems she never sends,
To almost lovers, fading friends.
Words unsaid but deeply felt,
In quiet corners where hearts melt.

A coffee cup, a rainy view,
A hoodie worn, a sky so blue.
She finds her peace in little things,
In broken notes and paper wings.

She dances when no one can see,
A quiet form of therapy.
No stage, no crowd, no need to prove—
Just her alone, allowed to move.

Her dreams are stitched in starlit thread,
In every tear she softly shed.
She falls, she breaks, she stands again,
A thousand times, through joy and pain.

Not just a girl in borrowed light,
But her own sun, burning bright.
No crown she needs, no throne to claim,
She writes herself beyond a name.

And when the world feels loud and cold,
She wraps herself in stories told.
Of who she was and who she'll be,
Of endless, wild identity.

For every girl who dares to feel,
Who hides her wounds yet learns to heal,
This is her song, her quiet art—
A universe inside her heart.
